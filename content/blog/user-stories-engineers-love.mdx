## Why Most User Stories Fail

The standard "As a [user], I want [feature], so that [benefit]" format is fine as a starting point. But most POs stop there, and that is where the problems begin...

## The Anatomy of a Great Story

A story that engineers love has five things beyond the basic format:

**1. Explicit acceptance criteria** — not vague, not aspirational. Testable.

**2. Clear out-of-scope** — what this story deliberately does NOT include.

**3. Edge cases addressed upfront** — what happens when the user does X instead of Y.

**4. Technical context** — any known constraints, existing patterns to follow, or APIs involved.

**5. A definition of done** — agreed before the sprint starts, not negotiated at review.
